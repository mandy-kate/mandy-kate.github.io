EDA First Impressions

Mandy Baker

21 May 2016

What's your take on EDA experience? What are your impressions?

The first week for me has been awesome, but also challenging. I enjoyed listening to Sheree's Fireside Chat; I thought it provided a good insight into the culture at EDA. I like the kitchen/creator mentality and it's good to be concious of this early in the course as I imagine it can be easy to slip out of this mindset - especially during times of stress. It's reassuring for me to think of our cohort as a team and being in this together. I value the diversity of the group and the learning opportunities that will come out of it. I appreciate the tension that the EDA teachers are going to experience as they determine what speed to teach at. I look forward to discovering new abilities and contributing to the group.

I am definitely excited to be at EDA. It seems to me like a great balance of challenge and enjoyment. Of course, I am also nervous about the intense challenge ahead. But I plan to turn up with integrity, effort and kindness and work to do my best.
